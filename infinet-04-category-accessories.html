Accessories and Cables

Carrier-class solutions to create high-capacity wireless infrustructures.
Type of product
Synchronization Unit AUX-ODU-SYNC
Is a TDD synchronization hub, which has been designed to provide a timing reference to InfiMAN 2x2 and InfiMAN Evolution base station sectors as well as to InfiLINK 2x2 PRO units
ANT-SYNC
ANT-SYNC is an active GPS/GLONASS antenna designed to filter and amplify RF signals received from global navigation satellite systems (GNSS) and feed them to a GNSS receiver at InfiLINK XG, InfiLINK XG 1000 families outdoor units. GNSS receiver recovers timing and positioning data which is used to provide timing reference for TDD synchronization.
Network switch InfiMUX 6G
Compact desktop network switch with MINT protocol support.
Indoor AC/DC injector with integrated lightning protection IDU-CPE
IDU-CPE is an indoor AC/DC PoE injector with integrated lightning protection. Compatible with R5000-Smn/R5000-Lmn units only.
Indoor AC/DC injector with integrated lightning protection IDU-BS-G
IDU-BS-G is an indoor AC/DC PoE injector with integrated lightning protection. Compatible with Omx/Mmx units, Smn/Lmn.
Indoor AC/DC injector with integrated lightning protection IDU-BS-G(60W)
IDU-BS-G(60W) is an indoor AC/DC PoE injector with integrated lightning protection. Compatible with all Infinet Wireless units.
