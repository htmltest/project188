Application

In the fast moving and highly competitive market of telecoms, operators are not only facing the challenge of competing with low cost VNOs (Virtual Network Operators) but are also struggling to retain their existing subscribers.

The provision of higher bandwidth for more and more customers requires technologies that deliver scalable capacity, long reach and ease-of-use. Infinet Wireless's high-capacity backhauls and multipoint solutions enable both wireless and wired service providers to meet their customers' growing needs for bandwidth, coverage and mobility.

In the case of a mobile operator, harsh competition as well as declining ARPUs (Average Revenue per User) has created a vital need to reduce network costs while delivering robust, high quality services. Backhauling traffic from cell sites is one of their biggest challenges.

Infinet Wireless has the solution to this challenge, ensuring that wireless carriers have the data capacity they need for both today’s and tomorrow’s growing bandwidth demands. It involves the migration towards a Carrier Ethernet, with switches/routers that multiplex circuit and data traffic from legacy base stations with new, native IP traffic. Infinet Wireless's next-generation radio solutions have in effect removed the air interface bottleneck. Quick and easy to deploy, they provide secure voice and data connections that are completely scalable and secure, offering carrier-class reliability and total reliability in harsh environmental conditions.

Mobile Backhaul Solutions

Mobile operators work in a world of stiff competition, as well as declining revenues per user. This has created a vital need to reduce network costs while delivering robust, high quality services. Backhauling traffic from cell sites is one of their biggest challenges.

Infinet Wireless offers unrivalled, high-capacity, point-to-point and point-to-multipoint products which are ideal for providing wireless backhaul solutions for mobile operators that are easily scalable and cost-effective. With the advent of 4G, Infinet Wireless’s solutions can provide high capacity mobile backhauls, while also supporting 3G and LTE, to provide reliable, high quality voice, video and data services.

For the highly competitive telecoms market, Infinet Wireless’s solutions provide significantly reduced deployment time over fibre installations and reduced upfront network investment thanks to its products’ flexibility and ease of deployment. In addition, InfiNet’s solutions are extremely robust and reliable, offer high throughput, long reach, seamless integration, a wide frequency range and QoS support.

This all means that end-user demand for bandwidth, coverage and mobility are met with ease and are future-proofed for tomorrow’s growing requirements. Quick and easy to deploy, Infinet Wireless's solutions provide secure voice and data connections that are completely flexible and secure, offering carrier-class reliability even in harsh environmental conditions.

NLOS backhaul

Infinet Wireless offers unrivalled, high-capacity, point-to-point and point-to-multipoint products which are ideal for providing wireless backhaul solutions in a Non-line-of-sight (NLOS) environment, that are easily scalable and cost-effective. The majority of wireless installations using InfiNet equipment have clear visibility from one end of the link to the other, with no obstacles. However, for many requirements, especially backhauls, clear line-of-sight is not possible.

In such cases, use of multiple antennas at both the transmitter and receiver side improves communication performance. InfiNet MIMO 2x2 technology effectively doubles spectrum efficiency and allows throughput of up to 280 Mbps.

The benefits of using Infinet Point-to-Point and Point-to-Multipoint solutions that provide FBWA systems with NLOS capabilities include:

  • Better coverage and penetration, which enables the provision of BWA services to previously unserved customers. This increases revenue opportunities for Operators and Service Provider.
  • Reduced operation and installation costs, thanks to faster and simpler installation procedures that do not dictate mandatory LOS conditions and may save the need to install additional accessory equipment such as high masts, etc.
  • Install-and-forget reliability.
